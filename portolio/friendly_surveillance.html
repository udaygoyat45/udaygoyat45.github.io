Friendly Surveillance

A CLI tool to help you stay focused on your tasks by using a local AI model to analyze your screen and provide feedback on your focus level.

Find the project on GitHub: udaygoyat45/friendly-surveillance

Features

  • Track your tasks with simple commands.
  • Get insights into your focus during a work session.
  • Uses a local AI model to respect your privacy (no data sent to the cloud).

Goal

The goal of this project is to explore how local AI can be used to create "friendly surveillance" tools that help people improve their focus and productivity in a privacy-preserving way.
